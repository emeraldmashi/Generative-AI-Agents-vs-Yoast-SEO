# Generative-AI-Agents-vs-Yoast-SEO
Master's Thesis Title - **"Technical Evaluation of the Use of Generative AI Agents in Digital Marketing Content Creation"**

**Objective**: To evaluate SEO of blogposts from Search Engine Journal (SEJ) vs bloposts created by Generative AI tools.

**Data Collection Method:**

**Step 01** - 
     Found 101 blogposts related to Digital Marketing on Search Engine Journal (SEJ).

**Step 02** - 
     Used Yoast Plugin to identify the score for SEO Insights (Flesch Reading Ease, Reading Time and Word Count) and readability parameters (passive voice, consecutive voice, paragraph length, sentence length and transition words) and check if they appear as a problem, improvement or good results.

**Non-guided Data Collection**------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Step 03** - 
     Copy the article title and searched  "Write a blogpost on 'article title'" from ChatGPT, Gemini, Blackbox AI and Grammarly Generative AI.

**Step 04** - 
    Repeat the Step 02.

**Guided Data Collection**----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Step 05** - 
     Search the following prompt on ChatGPT, Gemini, Blackbox AI and Grammarly Generative AI.
     Write a blogpost on "Original article title" that is SEO optimized with the following subtitles. "Orginal article subtitle 1", "Orginal article subtitle 2", "Orginal article subtitle 3", so on.

**Step 06** - 
    Repeat the Step 02.
